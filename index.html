I Develop Application that Help People

During the summer, I started working on a project focused on enhancing the daily lives of NYUAD students. The outcome was a cross-platform app designed to alert students promptly when their laundry is complete. Recognizing the common challenge of remembering laundry schedules, our app responds intelligently to various scenarios, keeping users informed about the status of their laundry.

This project reflects my commitment to practical problem-solving and improving the overall experience for my fellow students at NYUAD.
